About Us

We are India's second Largest University

The University Of North Bengal was established by an Act oif the Legislature of West Bengal in 1962 with the mission "to encourage and provide for instruction for teaching, training and research in various branches of learning and course of study; to promote advancement and dissemination of knowledge and learning and to extend higher education to meet the growing needs of society".
The university of North Bengal campus covers the area of nearly 315.99 acres of land at the foothills of high Himalaya in close proximity of vast palins around it with a fascinating glimpse of the kanchunjunga peak, tea gardens and forest covers.
